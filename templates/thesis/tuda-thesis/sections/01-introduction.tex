\addchap{Introduction}

\enquote{TUDaThesis.tex} is a template for theses using the Corporate Design of TU~Darmstadt.
It is part of TUDa-CI, the official template bundle of TU~Darmstadt.

\section{Features}

This template provides:
\begin{itemize}
  \item Full compliance with TU~Darmstadt's corporate design (\texttt{tudapub} class)
  \item Support for bachelor, master, and doctoral theses
  \item Integrated bibliography via \texttt{biblatex}/\texttt{biber} \cite{tuda-ci}
  \item PDF/A-2b output via \texttt{pdfmanagement}
\end{itemize}

\section{Usage}

Edit \texttt{frontmatter/metadata.tex} to set your name, title, department, and reviewers.
Add your content in the \texttt{sections/} directory and your references in
\texttt{bibliography/references.bib}.
